# Privacy Policy — Filament Tracker

**Last updated: June 10, 2026**

---

## Who we are

Filament Tracker is a business management application for 3D printing operations. It helps you track orders, models, and filament usage across your print jobs. The app is available as an Android mobile app.

---

## Information we collect

We only collect information you directly provide when using the app:

- **Account credentials** — your email address and a hashed password used to sign in.
- **Order data** — order names, customer names, and order status you enter.
- **Model data** — 3D model names, notes, and print quantities you record.
- **Filament usage** — filament type, colour, and gram weights you log against models.

We do **not** collect device identifiers, location data, usage analytics, advertising IDs, or any information beyond what you deliberately enter.

---

## How we use your information

Your data is used solely to operate the app for you:

- Authenticating your account and maintaining your session.
- Storing and displaying your orders, models, and filament records.
- Calculating filament summaries and usage totals.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the app's core functionality.

---

## Data storage and security

All data is stored in [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a managed cloud database platform hosted on AWS infrastructure. Your data is protected by:

- **Row-Level Security (RLS)** policies ensuring you can only access your own data.
- **Encrypted connections (TLS)** between the app and the database.
- **Password hashing** — your password is never stored in plain text.

You can review Supabase's security and privacy practices at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

---

## Data sharing

> **We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.**  
> Your business data is yours — it is never disclosed to advertisers, data brokers, or other services.

The only external service with access to your data is Supabase, acting as our infrastructure provider under a data processing relationship. No other third parties have access.

---

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual orders, models, and filament records at any time from within the app.

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

---

## Cookies and local storage

The app stores your session token in encrypted local storage on your device so you remain signed in between sessions. No data is written to shared or external storage.

---

## Children's privacy

Filament Tracker is intended for use by adults running 3D printing businesses. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
